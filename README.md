# 👻 Project Ghost

### Exposing Hidden Fraud Networks in Digital Banking

> *What if the biggest threat to a bank isn't a fraudulent account... but a network of accounts hiding in plain sight?*

---

## The Problem

Banks are exceptionally good at verifying identities.

Yet fraud continues to grow.

Why?

Because modern fraud rarely operates through a single account.

Fraudsters create multiple identities, use different devices, open accounts across institutions, route money through mule networks, and exploit the fact that banks often see only a small piece of the bigger picture.

Each account appears legitimate.

Each transaction appears normal.

But together, they form a hidden fraud ecosystem.

By the time traditional systems detect suspicious activity, financial losses have often already occurred.

---

## Meet Project Ghost

Project Ghost is a next-generation Identity Trust Network designed to uncover the invisible relationships that traditional banking security systems cannot see.<img width="1536" height="1024" alt="Architecture And Workflow Diagram Content" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fc9562e0-b05f-42b5-891a-e8ee942957fe" />


Instead of asking:

> **"Is this account suspicious?"**

Project Ghost asks:

> **"Who is connected to whom, and what are they hiding?"**

The platform continuously evaluates identity trust through behavioral intelligence, relationship mapping, device trust analysis, and adaptive risk scoring.

By transforming isolated banking events into a connected intelligence graph, Project Ghost exposes fraud networks before they can cause damage.

---

## How It Works

Project Ghost continuously analyzes:

* Login behavior
* Device fingerprints
* Transaction activity
* Beneficiary relationships
* Access patterns
* Location intelligence
* Account recovery events

These signals are transformed into a dynamic trust graph where identities, devices, accounts, and transactions become connected entities.

The system then identifies:

* Hidden fraud rings
* Account takeover attempts
* Suspicious onboarding activities
* Synthetic identities
* Insider misuse
* Cross-bank fraud patterns

When risk levels increase, Project Ghost triggers adaptive verification in real time while minimizing friction for legitimate users.

---

## What Makes Project Ghost Different?

Most fraud detection systems focus on **events**.

Project Ghost focuses on **relationships**.

Traditional systems see:

```text
Account A
Account B
Account C
```

Project Ghost sees:

```text
Account A ── Same Device ── Account B
       │
       └── Same Beneficiary ── Account C
```

The result?

Hidden fraud networks become visible.
